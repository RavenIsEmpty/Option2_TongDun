Who We Are

Tong Dun — Your Trusted Hardware Partner

Tong Dun specialises in production management of premium door hardware and building materials. We supply anti-theft locks, blade locks, traffic locks, ball locks, padlocks, car locks, handle locks, fingerprint locks, shutter locks, and a full variety of export-grade locks.

Our product range also includes high-quality single doors, twin entry doors, and porcelain floor tiles — everything you need to complete a home or commercial project. We stand by our motto: "Customer first, integrity first."
